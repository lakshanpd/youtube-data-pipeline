# YouTube Sri Lanka Analysis

## Overview

This project aims to analyze YouTube video trends and user behavior in Sri Lanka. The goal is to provide actionable insights about content popularity, audience engagement patterns, and creator activity. By examining YouTube data specifically for Sri Lanka, this project can help content creators, marketers, and analysts understand what works best in the local YouTube ecosystem.

## Goals

The project focuses on three main questions:

1. **Most Popular Video Categories**  
   Identify which categories of videos (e.g., music, education, entertainment, vlogs) are most popular among Sri Lankan viewers. Popularity will be measured through engagement metrics such as views, likes, and comments.

2. **User Activity Patterns**  
   Determine the time periods when users in Sri Lanka are most active on YouTube. This will help understand peak viewing hours and daily/weekly trends in audience behavior.

3. **Creator Upload Patterns**  
   Analyze the times when Sri Lankan YouTubers most frequently upload their videos. This will help identify content publishing trends and optimal upload times for reaching audiences.

## Expected Outcomes

By the end of this project, we aim to produce:

- Insights into top-performing video categories in Sri Lanka  
- Analysis of peak user activity hours and days  
- Patterns of content uploads by local creators  

These findings can be useful for content strategy, marketing decisions, and understanding YouTube behavior in the Sri Lankan context.
